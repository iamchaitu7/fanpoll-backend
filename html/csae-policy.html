Fanpollworld is committed to protecting children and vulnerable individuals from Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE). We have a zero-tolerance policy toward any content, activity, or behavior that involves exploitation or abuse.

Our Commitment

  • We prohibit uploading, sharing, or promoting CSAE content on our platform.
  • We actively monitor and report any suspected CSAE activity to appropriate authorities.
  • We cooperate fully with law enforcement and child protection organizations.

Reporting CSAE Concerns

If you encounter any suspicious content or behavior on Fanpollworld that could relate to CSAE, please report it immediately by emailing fanpollhub@gmail.com. Reports will be reviewed promptly and acted upon without delay.

Enforcement Actions

  • Immediate suspension or termination of accounts involved in CSAE.
  • Permanent removal of illegal or harmful content.
  • Referral of cases to law enforcement agencies.

Awareness & Prevention

We educate our team and users on the importance of online safety and remain updated with the latest global standards for protecting children online. Our safeguards are reviewed regularly to ensure compliance with international child protection frameworks.

Review & Updates

This CSAE Policy will be reviewed periodically and updated as needed to strengthen our protection measures. The latest version will always be available at: https://fanpollworld.com/csae-policy.html
